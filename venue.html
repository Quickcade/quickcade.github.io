RedBull Gaming Sphere London

The RedBull Gaming Sphere is located in Shoreditch, East London.
The address is: 2 Chance Street, London, E1 6JT

Accessibility

The main enterance to the venue is by a steep staircase. There is also an accessible lift. There is no parking at the venue, and surrounding roads are double yellow lined. We recommend using public transport where possible.
