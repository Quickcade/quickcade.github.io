Attending QuickCade II

Tickets

QuickCade is free to attend, though we ask that you register for a ticket. This lets us plan for the number of attendees. Registration and submissions are open now. Submissions will be open until February 4th, and registration remains open until the 1st of March. Please follow the links in the navigation bar to register and submit runs.

Under 16s are unfortunately not able to attend due to a venue restriction. If you will be 16 or above on the 7th of March 2020 then you are welcome join us.

Please be aware all attendees are required to have photographic ID with them. A photocopy of a passport or drivers licence is sufficient.

Venue

This year RedBull have kindly provided the RedBull Gaming Sphere in London.

The venue is located in Shoreditch, East London. The address is: 2 Chance Street, London, E1 6JT. The main entrance is actually on the adjacent Whitby Street.

The main entrance is via a steep staircase, though there is also an accessible lift available.

For assistance with travel, jump on the Discord #travel channel and let us know where you're coming from!

By Rail

Shoreditch High Street Overground station is a minutes walk from the venue, and is fully step free. Liverpool Street station is a 10 minute walk away, with many links to the rest of the network.

Check out the TFL Journey Planner or Citymapper for more info.

By Bus

The Shoreditch High Street bus stop is a minutes walk from the venue. There is a wide range of services that serve the stop.

Check out the TFL Journey Planner or Citymapper for more info.

By Car

We do not recommend you travel to the venue by car. If you need to please beware that the roads around the venue are narrow and cannot be parked on. The venue lies meters outside the congestion charge zone, so be very careful where you turn to avoid being charged.

If you're traveling from afar into London, you may want to consider parking further out and completing your journey by public transport. JustPark can be very useful for finding cheap parking.

Approaching from the west of London, the DoubleTree Ealing car park on JustPark is an easy drive, as is onward travel.

Accommodation

We have mass housing accommodation available in the form of two AirBnBs. There is an option for 3 nights, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday, for £38, and an option to stay just on the Saturday night for £18. Registration for accommodation can be found here.

If you can, please bring your own sleeping bag and air mattress. If you are travelling from far away we may be able to provide some for you. Please let us know on the Discord if you need us to provide bedding for you.
