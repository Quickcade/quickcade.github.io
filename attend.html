Tickets

QuickCade is free to attend, though we ask that you register for a ticket. This lets us plan for the number of attendees. Registration opens in the new year!

Under 16s are unfortunately not able to attend due to a venue restriction. If you will be 16 or above on the 7th of May 2020 then you are welcome join us.

Please be aware all attendees are required to have photographic ID with them. A photocopy of a passport of drivers licence is sufficient.
